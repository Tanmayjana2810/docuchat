Northwind Labs — Sample Company Handbook (for testing the Q&A app)

About
Northwind Labs is an AI development and strategy studio based in Austin, Texas. The
company builds Minimum Viable Products and helps organizations integrate AI into
their existing systems.

Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. The company
operates fully remote, and employees may choose their own schedule as long as
they overlap at least four hours with the US Central time zone.

Leave Policy
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off per year, plus 10 public
holidays. Unused leave can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the
next calendar year.

Equipment
Every new engineer receives a company laptop and a one-time 500 USD stipend for
a home-office setup. Equipment must be returned when leaving the company.

Code Review
All code must be reviewed by at least one other engineer before being merged to
the main branch. Continuous integration must pass before review is requested.

Security
Employees must enable two-factor authentication on all company accounts. API
keys and secrets must never be committed to a repository; use environment
variables instead.
